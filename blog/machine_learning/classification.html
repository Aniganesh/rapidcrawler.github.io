Precision & Recall:
Credits: deepai.org

Explanation: u/question_23
Imagine you're fishing and you have been informed there are a total of 100 fishes in the pond. Out of those 100 fishes present in the pond, you manage to catch 80

But you also get 80 rocks in your net. So, the total number of things retrived in your fish net is 80 Fishes + 80 Stones/Junk = 160 things

** Note: Both Precision & Recall are bothered about the retrived fish's count
However, Precision is about what proportion of things in net is fish
And, Recall is about what proportion of fish we were able to retrive from the pond

Precision & Recall Trade-off
You could use a smaller net and target one pocket of the lake where there are lots of fish and no rocks, but you might only get 20 of the fish in order to get 0 rocks.
That is 20% recall but 100% precision.

ROC & AUC Curve
Sensitivity / True Positive Rate / Recall: Sensitivity tells us what proportion of the positive class got correctly classified
Specificity / True Negative Rate: Specificity tells us what proportion of the negative class got correctly classified
False Negative Rate: FNR tells us what proportion of the positive class got incorrectly classified by the classifier
False Positive Rate: FPR tells us what proportion of the negative class got incorrectly classified by the classifier
