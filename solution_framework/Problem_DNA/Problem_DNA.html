First Principle Thinking

Derived from cognitive toolkit, also called as mental models, First Principle Thinking involves breaking down complex problems into their most basic, foundational elements and then reassembling them from the ground up. It helps to break down complex problems into their most basic elements and build up solutions from these foundational truths. It's about getting to the root of a problem.
