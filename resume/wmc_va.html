Solution Approach

How much business can we expect for coming month?

i) Built Neural Network based RNN Model,for this Sales Forecasting problem, to assist one of the Fortune 10 Retailer to leverage their historical sales data, in order to achieve weekly demand forecasting at Product-Store level
ii) Using this solution, client was able to minimize thier Out of Stock ratio, and thus reducing chances of missing out on high sales opportunities
iii) An increase in 6%-20% of accuracy as compared to previous models was achieved, for different kinds of product categories
